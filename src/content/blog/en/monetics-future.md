The e-payment sector is in the midst of a major transformation. With the advent of contactless payments, cryptocurrencies, and digital wallets, traditional systems must adapt rapidly while guaranteeing a maximum level of security.

## New Payment Standards

Electronic payment terminals (POS) are evolving towards software solutions (SoftPOS) where a simple smartphone can serve as a terminal. This poses enormous security challenges, as the execution environment (the merchant's phone) is no longer controlled or physically secure.

## Tokenization

To limit the risk of fraud, tokenization has become the norm. Rather than transmitting the actual card number (PAN), a single-use or restricted token is generated. Thus, even in the event of interception, the data is unusable by an attacker.
